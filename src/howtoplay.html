comptetitype.

competitype is a game where you compete with other players to see who can type the most words within their stamina.

how to play?

  1. Press the start button. Be ready as your cursor will be instantly focused on the input box!
  2. The timer will start at 5 seconds. Per word you type correctly, you will gain 1.5 seconds to the timer.
  3. Type as many words as you can as the display updates. It will notify you if you have a letter correct or wrong.
  4. Enjoy!

You have the choice to submit your name and score to the leaderboard.
